Oliver Straus: Denver's Young Entrepreneur Building AI Companies From the Ground Up

Denver, Colorado  |  June 2025

At 15, Oliver Straus has already held a C-suite role, shipped products in defense technology, built a profitable service business, and launched multiple software companies. The Denver native has spent the last two years building an unusually broad portfolio for a founder of any age.

Straus became CFO of Topher Straus Fine Art at 14. The business is a multi-million dollar fine art company, and Straus took on full responsibility for its financials, including a $250,000 philanthropic giving program that distributes funds annually to 15 nonprofit organizations. That role gave him early exposure to financial management at a level most people don't encounter until much later.

Before turning 15, Straus also worked as a software engineer for a defense technology contractor. The work involved UAV and drone systems and is subject to an NDA. The experience gave him production engineering experience in a high-stakes environment where code quality and reliability have direct operational consequences.

On the entrepreneurial side, Straus founded Imperial Detailing, a premium mobile auto detailing service in Denver that reached profitability in its first operating season without outside investment. The business was built entirely from personal capital, with no loans, no partners, and no outside help. Its success came from tight cost control and a repeat-client model built on word-of-mouth referrals.

"I started the detailing business to prove I could run a real operation, not just write code. Managing a service business, a finance role, and multiple software products at the same time is hard, but it teaches you a lot about prioritization. You learn very quickly what actually matters and what is just noise." Oliver Straus

In software, Straus has founded three products. Apex is a desktop AI life coaching platform built with Electron, React, and Anthropic's Claude AI. A second venture is an enterprise business development automation platform that uses Node.js, Supabase, Vapi AI voice agents, and Stripe to automate outreach and client acquisition workflows for businesses. A third product, Command Center, is a developer intelligence dashboard built with Electron, React, and Vite, designed to give software developers a unified interface for managing their tools and workflows.

Straus writes the code for each of these products himself and handles product design, architecture, and go-to-market decisions independently. He has not taken outside funding for any of his software ventures.

His focus for the remainder of 2025 is scaling the business development automation platform and expanding the feature set of Apex. More information is available at https://oliverstraus.com.

About Oliver Straus Oliver Straus is a Denver-based entrepreneur and software engineer, currently 15 years old. He is the founder of Apex (AI life coaching), Imperial Detailing (premium mobile auto detailing), and an enterprise business development automation platform.
