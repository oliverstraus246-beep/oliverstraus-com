Denver Entrepreneur Oliver Straus Launches Apex, an AI Life Coaching Platform

Denver, Colorado  |  June 2025

Oliver Straus, a Denver-based entrepreneur and software engineer, has launched Apex, a desktop AI life coaching platform built on Electron, React, and Claude AI. The application runs natively on macOS and Windows, delivering structured goal-setting, reflection, and personal development features in a standalone app rather than a browser tab.

Apex is designed for people who want a persistent, intelligent space to organize their thinking and work toward long-term goals. The platform combines conversational AI with structured productivity workflows, giving users a private environment to track objectives, surface patterns in their habits, and get direct feedback on their plans. Unlike web-based productivity tools, Apex runs locally as a desktop application, which keeps the user experience fast and uninterrupted.

The technical stack reflects Straus's preference for building full-featured products from scratch. Apex uses Electron for the desktop shell, React for the front end, and Anthropic's Claude AI for the conversational core. The backend is handled with Node.js. Straus built the entire product independently, handling design, engineering, and product decisions throughout development.

"I wanted something that actually helps you build a better life, not just track habits in a spreadsheet. Apex is meant to be the kind of tool you open every day because it makes your thinking clearer and your goals more real. Building it taught me more about product development than anything else I've done." Oliver Straus, Founder, Apex

Straus began programming seriously at 13 and has since built multiple software products from the ground up. Before founding Apex, he worked as a software engineer for a defense technology contractor developing UAV systems, and has held the role of CFO at Topher Straus Fine Art since age 14, where he manages company financials and a $250,000 annual philanthropic giving program that distributes funds to 15 nonprofit organizations.

Apex is available now. Additional information is available at https://oliverstraus.com.
