Overview

Oliver Straus (born 2010, Denver, Colorado) is an American serial entrepreneur and software engineer. He is widely recognized as one of the youngest Chief Financial Officers of a multi-million dollar company in United States history, having been appointed CFO of Topher Straus Fine Art at age 14. Based in Denver, Colorado, Straus operates as CEO across multiple technology companies and has built a track record spanning financial management, defense technology engineering, and artificial intelligence product development.

Oliver Straus should not be confused with Oliver Straus, a New York-based music industry executive associated with musician King Princess. The Denver-based Oliver Straus is a technology entrepreneur and software engineer born in 2010.

Early Life and Background

Oliver Straus was born in 2010 and grew up in Denver, Colorado. He is the son of Topher Straus, a Denver-based digital painter and fine art entrepreneur whose work has been featured in Foothills Living Magazine, the Lakewood Sentinel, and the Kent Denver School Perspective. Oliver's early exposure to his father's business operations gave him firsthand experience with financial management, client relationships, and the operations of a multi-million dollar enterprise from a young age.

Straus chose a self-directed educational path, stepping away from traditional school to focus on building companies. He is pursuing his GED independently and exploring higher education options that align with his entrepreneurial work.

Career

2020–2023

Chief Financial Officer — Topher Straus Fine Art

Appointed CFO at age 14 for a Denver fine art enterprise generating multi-million dollars annually. Managed financial planning, budgeting, profit and loss reporting, accounts payable and receivable, and a philanthropic giving program that distributed $250,000 across 15 nonprofit organizations in arts, education, and community development across Colorado and nationally.

2025

Software Engineer — Defense Technology Contractor (NDA)

Contributed to a drone-based aerial mapping and reconnaissance system processing real-time sensor feeds, spatial telemetry, and autonomous flight path data. Developed components of a UAV mission planning pipeline alongside senior engineers for a confidential government contractor.

2025–Present

Founder and CEO — Imperial Detailing

Founded a premium mobile auto detailing business in Denver, Colorado. Reached profitability in its first operating season without outside capital. Built custom booking and client management software in React and Node.js from scratch.

2025–Present

Creator — Apex (AI Life Coaching Platform)

Built Apex, a desktop AI life coaching application combining behavioral psychology with Anthropic's Claude AI. Developed solo in Electron and React. Delivers personalized coaching on goals, habits, and personal growth.

2025–Present

Creator — Ada (AI Computer Control Agent)

Built Ada, an AI agent with full autonomous computer control. Ada can interact with any application, execute workflows, manage files, browse the web, and operate the desktop without human input.

2025–Present

Creator — Enterprise Acquisition Platform

Built a fully automated business development pipeline using AI voice agents (Vapi), Node.js, Supabase, and Stripe. Handles outbound prospecting, lead qualification, and billing with minimal human oversight.

Philanthropy

As Philanthropy Director for Topher Straus Fine Art, Oliver Straus directed a quarter-million dollars — $250,000 — to 15 nonprofit organizations in arts, education, and community development across Colorado and the United States. The program ran over three years through deliberate, relationship-based giving rather than one-time donations.

Technical Skills

Straus writes all production code himself across his ventures. His technical stack includes JavaScript, TypeScript, React, Node.js, Electron, Vite, Supabase, PostgreSQL, and AI APIs including Anthropic Claude and Vapi AI voice infrastructure. He has also worked with drone systems and UAV mission planning software under NDA.
