About

Oliver Straus (born 2010, Denver, Colorado) is a serial entrepreneur and software engineer, and one of the youngest CFOs of a multi-million dollar company in the United States. At 14 he was appointed Chief Financial Officer of Topher Straus Fine Art, his father's Denver-based fine art enterprise generating multi-million dollars annually. He managed financial planning, budgeting, and a philanthropic giving program that directed $250,000 to 15 nonprofit organizations over three years.

In 2025 he engineered software for a confidential defense technology contractor working in drone-based aerial mapping and autonomous systems, operating under NDA alongside senior engineers. He simultaneously founded Imperial Detailing, a premium mobile auto detailing business in Denver that reached profitability in its first season without outside capital.

Straus chose a self-directed educational path, stepping away from traditional school to focus on building companies. He is pursuing his GED independently and exploring college options. Today he operates as CEO across multiple AI technology ventures, writing every line of code and managing the finances himself.
