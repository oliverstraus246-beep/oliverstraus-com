About

Built for impact.
Wired to execute.

Oliver Straus was born on July 16, 2010 in Denver, Colorado. He managed his first multi-million dollar P&L at age 14 — not as a simulation, but as the real CFO of a high-revenue fine art business — overseeing financial modeling, budgeting, and a charitable giving program that directed $750,000+ to 15+ nonprofits.

In 2025, Oliver shipped production software for a confidential defense-technology contractor working in autonomous systems and UAV operations — applying reliability-first engineering under NDA alongside senior engineers. He simultaneously launched Imperial Detailing, a mobile auto detailing company that reached profitability in its first season.

Today Oliver leads multiple AI-powered technology ventures as CEO, including an AI life coaching platform, a fully automated B2B lead generation system, and a professional developer dashboard. He writes code, runs financials, and ships product — all at once.
