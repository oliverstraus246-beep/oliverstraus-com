About

Youngest CFO in the room.
Most capable builder at the table.

Oliver Straus was born on July 16, 2010 in Denver, Colorado. At 14, he became the CFO of a multi-million dollar fine art enterprise, overseeing financial planning, budgeting, and a charitable giving program that directed $250,000 to 15 nonprofit organizations.

In 2025, Oliver shipped production software for a confidential defense-technology contractor working in autonomous systems and UAV operations. Simultaneously, he founded Imperial Detailing, a mobile auto detailing company in Denver that reached profitability in its first season without outside funding.

Today he leads multiple AI-powered technology ventures as CEO. He writes the code, manages the finances, and ships the product. All at once.
