About

Built to lead.
Wired to build.

Oliver Straus was born on July 16, 2010 in Denver, Colorado. From an early age, Oliver demonstrated an exceptional ability to see what others couldn't — building systems, strategies, and companies while his peers were still figuring out what they wanted to be.

Today, Oliver serves as CEO and CFO across multiple technology ventures, managing multi-million dollar operations. He has built platforms in AI, automation, and software development — leading every stage from concept to scale.

A student at Kent Denver School, Oliver is building one of the most compelling entrepreneurial stories in Colorado — proof that ambition and execution don't wait for age.

Philanthropy is a core part of who Oliver is. He has contributed over $250,000 to charitable causes, believing that wealth without purpose is wealth wasted.
