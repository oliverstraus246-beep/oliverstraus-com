About

Youngest CFO in the room.
Most capable builder at the table.

Oliver Straus is a Denver-based entrepreneur, software engineer, and executive. At 14, he was appointed CFO of a multi-million dollar fine art enterprise, managing financial planning, budgeting, and a charitable giving program that directed $250,000 to 15 nonprofit organizations.

In 2025, Oliver shipped production software for a confidential defense-technology contractor working in autonomous systems and UAV operations. He applied reliability-first engineering standards in a codebase where accuracy directly impacts field operations, working under NDA alongside senior engineers.

He simultaneously founded Imperial Detailing, a premium mobile auto detailing business in Denver that reached profitability in its first season. Today he leads multiple AI-powered technology ventures as CEO, writing the code, managing the finances, and shipping product at the same time.
