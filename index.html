About

Oliver Straus began his financial career at 14 as CFO of Topher Straus Fine Art, a Denver-based fine art business generating multi-million dollars annually. In that role he managed financial planning, budgeting, and accounts payable and receivable for three years, and built a philanthropic giving program that distributed $250,000 across 15 nonprofit organizations in arts, education, and community support.

In 2025 he contributed to a confidential drone-based aerial mapping and reconnaissance system, working under NDA alongside senior engineers on UAV mission planning software used across multiple governments. He simultaneously founded Imperial Detailing, a premium mobile auto detailing service in Denver that reached profitability in its first season without outside capital.

Today Straus operates as CEO across multiple AI technology companies. He writes the production code, manages the finances, and ships the products independently.
